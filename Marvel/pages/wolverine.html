Wolverine

Wolverine is a mutant with superhuman abilities. He has a regeneration that allows him to survive after severe wounds, lethal to an ordinary person. Most poisons and diseases also can not kill and cause serious damage to Wolverine's health. His ability also increases stamina and agility, sharpens his senses, slows down the aging of the body. The Wolverine's skeleton differs from human by the presence of six blades, similar to daggers and sharp as razors. Blades extend from the membranes between the fingers (each blade has three blades). After participating in the "Weapon X" experiment, Wolverine's skeleton was covered with an indestructible Adamantium alloy. Wolverine is an expert in many types of hand-to-hand combat, has combat training, worked as a scout, mercenary and CIA agent. Participated in the First and Second World Wars, fought in Spain and Vietnam, a great tactician and strategist.
